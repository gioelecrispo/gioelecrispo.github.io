Know Me More

I was born in 1993 in Salerno, Italy. Passionate about technology and electronics since childhood, I always want to experiment and learn. I graduated with honors in Computer Engineering and I am continuously learning on topics related to Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence in general.

Today, I work as a Machine Learning Engineer, focused on Natural Language Processing topics. As a team leader on several projects, I have always demonstrated discrete leadership and coordination skills and also excellent analytical, design and team working capabilities. I consider myself attentive to details and precise in meeting deadlines.
