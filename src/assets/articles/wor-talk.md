# A novel Writing Order Recovery approach for handwriting specimens

## Introduction
Tracing complex and long handwritten signatures takes an important role in signature verification. 
Indeed, whether a dynamic signature could be inferred from its static counterpart, improvements would be 
expected during the automatic verification.

There are two main modalities for Automatic Signature Verification (ASV) according to the type of signature: on-line
and off-line signatures. Because of the use of the temporal information, ASV systems based on on-line signatures offer
a more competitive performance than ASV ones based on off-line signatures [1]. Off-line signatures are based on static
images, whereas on-line signatures are registered in form of signals, which provide the kinematics of the movement. 
Therefore, recovering the dynamics of an image-based signature would lead to a relevant improvement, 
since on-line ASV can be used.
